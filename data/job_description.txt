Junior AI / Machine Learning Engineer

We are looking for a curious Junior AI / Machine Learning Engineer to join our
applied AI team. You will help design, evaluate, and maintain practical machine
learning features, with an emphasis on reproducible experiments and clear
technical communication.

Responsibilities
- Prepare and analyze structured and unstructured data with Python, pandas, and NumPy.
- Build and evaluate Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing solutions.
- Develop reproducible experiments and model pipelines with scikit-learn.
- Help expose model functionality through well-documented REST APIs.
- Use Git for version control, review, and collaborative development.
- Explore responsible applications of Generative AI under senior guidance.
- Document assumptions, evaluation results, limitations, and data-quality issues.

Required qualifications
- 0 to 2 years of professional or project experience in data science, AI, or software engineering.
- Bachelor of Technology, Bachelor of Engineering, or equivalent education in Computer Science or a related technical discipline.
- Strong foundations in Python and Machine Learning.
- Practical familiarity with scikit-learn, pandas, NumPy, Git, and REST APIs.
- Basic understanding of Natural Language Processing and Generative AI.
- Ability to communicate findings and write maintainable, testable code.

Nice to have
- Experience with FastAPI, Flask, SQL, Docker, Streamlit, or cloud platforms.
- Personal, academic, or internship projects involving text analysis or model evaluation.

This is a junior role. We value sound fundamentals, careful reasoning, learning
ability, and responsible engineering more than exposure to every listed tool.
